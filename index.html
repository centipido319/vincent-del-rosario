Sari Sari Savor: Recipe Finder App

Repository

Sari Sari Savor: Recipe Finder App is a college project I developed using Ionic, designed to simplify meal planning. The app allows users to input any ingredients they have in their pantry, and it instantly generates a list of recipes that can be made with those items.

This feature makes it convenient for people to reduce food waste and discover new meals without the need for additional shopping. Sari Sari Savor offers a practical solution for busy individuals looking to maximize their ingredients while exploring a variety of dishes.

Barker Kiosk

Barker Kiosk is a college project I developed as part of my coursework, designed to streamline the bus passenger booking experience. The system allows passengers to easily book their rides through a user-friendly kiosk interface. The name "Barker" is inspired by the traditional role of a barker, who calls out to passengers to board the bus.

By automating the booking process, Barker Kiosk enhances efficiency and reduces wait times, making transactions quicker and more convenient for passengers. This project not only showcases my technical skills but also highlights my commitment to improving transportation services through innovative solutions.

Christ Faith Child Academy Identification Card

I created and designed the Christ Faith Child Academy ID card, which effectively combines practicality with a design that reflects the school’s faith-based values. The front prominently displays the student’s name, photo, grade level, and Learner Reference Number against a backdrop of the academy. The school’s logo and address enhance its professional appearance.

On the reverse side, shows the importance of wisdom and faith in the academy’s philosophy. Additionally, it includes an emergency contact section and essential usage guidelines for safety. The layout is clean, well-organized, and visually appealing, making it both functional and representative of the school’s mission.
