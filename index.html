ABOUT ME

As a graduate with a BS in Information Technology, I specialize in web development, blending creativity with technical expertise to build engaging and functional websites. Throughout my journey, I’ve gained hands-on experience in web-based portal development and hardware requirements, bolstered by an OJT at a web development company. My skills range from coding and problem-solving to delivering user-friendly digital solutions. Driven by a passion for continuous learning and growth, I aim to develop innovative digital experiences that make a real impact. Let's collaborate and bring your ideas to life!

Design

Design goes beyond appearance—it's about creating something that works inside and out. How the design functions and how usersfeel interacting with it. My goal is to craft digital experiences and interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also enjoyable and intuitive.
